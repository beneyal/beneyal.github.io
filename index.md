Hello! :wave:

I'm Ben, a research engineer at [Ai2](https://allenai.org/). Right now I'm building [Paper Finder](https://asta.allen.ai), an agentic search tool that helps researchers navigate the scientific literature — it's used by thousands of people every month, which still feels pretty good to say.

Before that I worked on [SPIKE](https://spike.apps.allenai.org), a linguistic search engine built on top of deep NLP annotations. And before Ai2, I spent several years at Ben-Gurion University doing a PhD on Text-to-SQL parsing, teaching programming languages to undergrads, and generally thinking hard about how humans and computers communicate.

My publications are available [here](/publications/), and under [Teaching](/teaching/) you'll find supplementary material from courses I've lectured and TA'ed.
